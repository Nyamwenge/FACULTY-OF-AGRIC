VISSION:

The faculty of agriculture is to holistically build a critical
mass of quality professional agriculturalists with sustainable resource
management hands-on-skills, inorder to meet the university's goals
of prioirity,while contributing to the national goal of modernizing agriculture
as a means of achieving food security, alleviating poverty and diversifying
the economy.

The faculty programs are reviewed every three years to make them academically relevant in farming communities and competitive in the job market. Graduates find employment in a wide range of organizations in government, non-governmental organizations and private sectors. Key areas of specialization include; agribusiness and post-harvest handling of agricultural commodities and environmental management in the ecologically fragile environment.

We are proud of a well-equipped trained academic staff who are highly qualified and who continue to provide the right quality training and provide consultancy services and collaborate with internal organizations and other universities.
